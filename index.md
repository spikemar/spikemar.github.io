## Welcome!
I was born in Turkey, moved to the USA, moved back to Turkey, now I am doing a PhD at Boston University. I have a fairly wide range of interests but my education is in Electrical and Computer Engineering, primarily in computer design from the circuit level up. My bachelors were in physics and electrical and electronics engineering at Bogazici University Turkey (completed 2016). I have recently been picked as best graduate teaching fellow for Boston University (2017-2018). I have also been selected as a DAC Richard Newton Young student fellow and I was a recipient of a student scholarship to take part in the 50th ACM A.M. Turing Award conference. A copy of my CV  can be found [here](https://github.com/spikemar/spikemar.github.io/blob/master/docs/cv.pdf).
